Pollo Saltado

Description

Pollo saltado, a cherished Peruvian dish, features stir-fried chicken marinated in soy sauce, vinegar, and spices, wok-tossed with assorted vegetables. Its name, "pollo saltado," translates to "jumped chicken," a nod to the lively pan-tossing and fusion of flavors during cooking.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Bell peppers
  • Aji Amarillo

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken in soy sauce, vinegar, cumin, garlic, and aji amarillo.
  2. Cook chicken until done and set aside.
  3. Saut√© onions, bell peppers until tender.
  4. Combine cooked chicken and tomatoes in the pan.
  5. Season, garnish with cilantro, and serve over fries and rice.
