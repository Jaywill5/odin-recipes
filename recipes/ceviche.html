Ceviche

Description

Ceviche, a popular Latin American dish, features fresh seafood "cooked" by marinating it in citrus juices like lime or lemon, alongside ingredients such as onions, cilantro, and chili peppers, resulting in a bright and refreshing flavor.

Ingredients

  • Fresh seafood or fish
  • Citrus juices (lime or lemon)
  • Onions
  • Cilantro
  • Chili peppers

Steps

  1. Cut the fresh seafood or fish into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Place the seafood in a bowl.
  3. Squeeze lime or lemon juice over the seafood.
  4. Add diced onions, cilantro, and sliced chili peppers to the bowl.
  5. Allow the mixture to marinate in the citrus juices until the seafood is "cooked" by the acid.
