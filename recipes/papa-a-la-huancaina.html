Papa a la Huancaina

Description

Papa a la Huancaína is a Peruvian dish with boiled yellow potatoes served with a creamy, mildly spicy sauce made from fresh cheese and yellow chili pepper.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Yellow chili peppers
  • Fresh cheese
  • Evaporated milk
  • Saltine crackers

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes until tender, then peel and slice them.
  2. Blend queso fresco, aji amarillo, saltine crackers, and evaporated milk to make the Huancaína sauce.
  3. Pour the sauce over the potato slices.
  4. Garnish with boiled eggs, black olives, and chopped herbs.
  5. Chill and serve. Enjoy!
